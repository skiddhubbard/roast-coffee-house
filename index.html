A Dallas Landmark since 1973, at Roast, we believe Quality Starts From the Ground Up

Direct Trade Coffee

We don’t buy our coffee in bulk from just any old farm that makes us a low-priced offer. We seek out only the highest quality plantations around the world to bring you the best Direct Trade Certified coffees. Working with our Direct Trade Partners gives us great joy, and it is a passion we desire to continue and share with you.

Peak Roasting

You can’t get coffee much fresher than what is prepared directly by master coffee roasters in The Star of Texas. We always roast our coffee fresh. Once it arrives from one of the farms that we have partnered with around world, it rests in our warehouse for the least amount of time possible before being roasted and sent off to one of our Dallas-local stores or awesome wholesale customers.

Single Cup Brewing

At Roast, you’ll always get the highest quality coffee from seed to cup. From picking the first coffee seed to roasting it in our Dallas-based warehouse to brewing it by hand in one of our stores, we take a lot of pride in preparing coffee for you. When you come in to one of our stores, you’ll understand why we think coffee isn’t just about getting a cup to drink— it’s about having a great experience.

Roasts

Custom House Blend

A touch of french roast combined with two parts of nutty, bright colombian excelso and added costa rican tarrazu for sweetness.

Madagascar Vanilla

This creamy coffee melds vanilla, caramel and scotch whisky flavors for a unique and tasty experience.

South Pacific Blue

Our south pacific blue possesses a full, rich, sweet body reminiscent of coffees grown on the kona coast of Hawaii. The coffee in this area began with seedlings from the blue mountain region of Jamaica, during the 1930's.

Genuine Ethiopian Harrar Horse

Dried in the sun the traditional way, allowing the berries to slowly dry in the hot sun, the blueberry and raisin flavors fully develop and create subtle high notes to this coffee.

Red Eye Kenya

Red eye comes from the nyeri region and always delivers a wonderfully aromatic cup. When we opened our first airport location a few years back we were looking for the perfect coffee to get travelers going at 4:30am. Red eye was the perfect answer.

Colombian Mesa De Los Santos

Certified fair trade, organic, and bird friendly, this Colombian coffee is shade grown. It has a light to medium body and great balance, containing a natural sweetness preserved by the shade with hints of cinnamon, nuts, and chocolate.
